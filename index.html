About us

Security Communication Solutions International (SCSI) continues to pioneer advancements in wireless alarm monitoring technology, driven by an unwavering commitment to innovation. From the groundbreaking development of the Guardcell Wireless Alarm Interface to the success of our acclaimed 4G DirectWireless Network, SCSI has evolved to become Australia's leading provider of advanced 4G and IoT communication solutions for the security industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver innovative and reliable wireless communication solutions that enhance security and connectivity. We are dedicated to providing seamless, hassle-free alarm monitoring and advanced IoT technologies through our exclusive private networks, empowering our clients with peace of mind and exceptional service.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading force in transforming security communication by pioneering cutting-edge technologies and dedicated private networks. We aspire to shape a safer, more connected world where advanced communication solutions offer unparalleled protection and convenience for businesses and individuals alike.
